A Future Vet's Mission to Make Safe Wearable Art for Dogs

It all started with a simple question: Why does pet safety have to look so boring?

As a lifelong animal lover about to start my journey into veterinary school, I've always wanted to be surrounded by animals. Whether I was working hard at Colchester Zoo soaking up the vibrant wildlife atmosphere, or getting stuck into the hands-on care of beloved family pets at the local dog kennels, I learned firsthand just how unique every single animal's personality is. Our dogs give us so much joy, so why are they all wearing the exact same mass-produced, purely utilitarian collars? I wanted to create something that let pets show off their distinct spirit without ever compromising on their well-being.

That's how the idea of interchangeable collar charms was born. But turning a sketch into a safe reality meant doing things the right way. Driven by my love for animals and a lot of determination, I threw myself into the deep end of product development at 19. I spent months researching the market, studying materials, and designing a charm system built strictly around rigorous pet safety standards.

I didn't take shortcuts. I ordered prototypes, tested early samples in real-world conditions, and vetted dozens of manufacturers until I found the perfect partner who shared my commitment to quality.

Today, every single piece we sell is a labour of love, designed to bring a little extra joy to your daily walks. Thank you for supporting a young entrepreneur, a future vet, and most importantly, a fellow dog devotee. Let's make the world a little brighter, one charm at a time!
