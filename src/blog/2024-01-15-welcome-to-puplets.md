## Hello from Puplets!

We're thrilled to launch Puplets - where every collar tells a story and celebrates your dog's unique personality.

### Our Mission

As a veterinary student at the University of Bristol who works part-time at Colchester Zoo, I've always been passionate about animal welfare and creativity. Puplets was born from this love, combining quality craftsmanship with playful design.

### What Makes Puplets Special?

**Customization**: Choose from vibrant colors (Chilli Red, Ocean Blue, Fern Green), perfect-fit sizes (XS, S, M), and over 20 adorable charm designs.

**Quality**: Every collar is handcrafted with durable, comfortable materials that are built to last.

**Free Charm**: Each collar comes with one charm of your choice - because every pup deserves something special.

**Free Shipping**: We offer free delivery on all UK orders.
